Almond Milk Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 16oz bottles of water
  • 1 cup of raw almonds
  • Nutmeg or High-quality strainer
  • Blender
  • Cinnamon powder and/or dates
  • Glass ~30 oz milk bottle

NOTE: Raw almonds must be soaked in filtered water for 8 hrs minimum

Step-by-step

  1. Rinse almonds with water then put them in blender
  2. Add 2 bottles of water into blender, then blend for 90sec
  3. Pour almond milk through nutmeg/strainer to remove excess almonds onto a large bowl
  4. Once all excess almonds is removed, pour almond milk in blender (and add cinnamon powder/dates - optional) then blend for 30 sec
  5. Pour almond milk through strainer onto bowl then pour into milk in glass milk bottle
  6. ENJOY!
