Grilled Garlic Butter Shrimp

Ingredients

  • 8-12 shrimps
  • Butter
  • 1 clove of garlic

NOTE: Season shrimps w/ salt, pepper, garlic powder and old bay before starting step-by-step

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Wait until skillet gets hot, then cover it with butter
  2. Put shrimps on skillet
  3. Put some butter on the middle of skillet again, then put crushed garlic onto it then mix it with shrimps
  4. Cook until golden color on both sides, then enjoy!
